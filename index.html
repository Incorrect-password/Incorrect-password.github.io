About Me

Through Mayden I have received experience with all the software you would expect of a full stack developer with a focus on OOP and MVC in a LAMP stack. It also included working with RESTful APIs, TypeScript, SCSS and Bootstrap to name a few. I am a Certified Scrum Master with multiple agile projects under my belt and I am always looking for my next challenge so get in touch if you have one for me.

Having come through Mayden Academy at the ripe old age of 30 I feel I can bring to bear half a life time of experiences and the passion of someone who has found their calling in life.

I have a degree in Zoology and experience as an Ecologist both in the U.K. and Australia. I am also a qualified bicycle mechanic, fire warden, first aider, 4wd driver, experienced rock climber, cyclist and a keen naturalist, currently with a particular interest in Fungi.

Spot The Dog
IndexedDB PWA

My friends requested an app they could use without the internet that they could use to record all of the different dogs they see when they are out and about.

I undertook it in my own time so as to experiment with localforage.js and IndexedDB. If you open it with Ecosia it will prompt you to add it as an app, with Firefox it provides you with a button next to the address bar and in Chrome add it to your homepage through the menu.

Academy Portal

As a test of our abilities to follow MVC principles using Slim we were tasked as working as an agile team to add new features and improve the Mayden Academy portal that the Academy use to process their new applicants, hiring partners and events. We used PHP and MySQL for the back end programming and JS on the front end.

I used paired programming to add a modal to the hiring partners so that they could be displayed with the different contacts at the company. We also undertook a number of hotfixes as the ability to add new applicants and hiring partners had been compromised.

Mario Mania
RESTful API

We were tasked to build a RESTful API that can record players and results for Mario Kart. It can add new users, record results and you can request results for each track.

I was the Scrum Master for the project and though were only a small team we stuck the Agile principles and scrum framework. We used Node.js to create the API and kept it as RESTful as possible. We decided to use MySQL due to the fact that we had relational data which was an interesting change with Node.js as we had only used MongoDB with it in the past.

  • Scrum Master
  • Node.js
  • MySQL
  • Github

Finance Calculator
PWA

I decided to make a finance calculator for future Mayden Academy students so that they could easily calculate how much they would have to pay back if they borrowed money to pay for the course.

I made it a PWA so that they could use it as a lightweight app that would still function even if they did not have access to the internet. It is fully responsive and even has a hideaway menu in the mobile version.
